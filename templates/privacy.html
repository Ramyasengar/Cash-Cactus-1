Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how our app collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal and financial information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide a smooth and secure experience, which may include:

  • Personal details such as name, email address, and contact information
  • Financial data including income, expenses, and savings entered by the user
  • Transaction data retrieved securely through banking API integrations (with user consent)
  • App usage data to improve performance and user experience

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Track and analyze income, expenses, and savings
  • Generate visual charts and financial insights
  • Monitor spending limits and send alerts when monthly budgets are close to being exceeded
  • Provide guidance and recommendations for smart investing options
  • Improve app features, security, and overall functionality

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. All sensitive data is encrypted and processed through secure systems to ensure confidentiality and integrity.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Information is shared only:

  • With trusted service providers (such as banking APIs) strictly for functionality
  • When required by law or legal authorities

5. User Control & Consent

You have full control over your data. You may:

  • Review, update, or delete your personal information
  • Enable or disable banking integrations at any time
  • Withdraw consent for data processing where applicable

6. Cookies & Analytics

The app may use cookies or analytics tools to understand usage patterns and improve user experience. These tools do not collect personally identifiable information without consent.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated within the app, and continued use of the app implies acceptance of the updated policy.
